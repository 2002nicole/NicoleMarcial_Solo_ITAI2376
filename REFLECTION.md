I did not switch my original decision (single agent) because I think working with a single agent would work better for me based on my skills. If I were to do a multi-agent I would probably overcomplicate stuff for myself.

What you would build next if you had another semester.
I think if I had to do another agent, I would probably do one incorporating cakes and frostings. 
There's a lot of things that comes with baking cakes such as pan size, baking time, layers, fillings, frosting/buttercream, servings, flavors.
I think creating an agent that (just like this cookie agent) takes into account all of that and helps a beginner in making decisions based on their goals would be very useful.
